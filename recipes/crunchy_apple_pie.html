Crunchy Apple Pie

Description

Sweet and crunchy apple pie.

Ingredients

  • 150 g butter
  • 2 dl unrefined sugar
  • 0.5 dl golden syrup
  • 0.5 dl cream
  • 3 dl rolled oats
  • 1.5 dl flour
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp flake salt
  • 600 g apples

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Mix oats, flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Melt butter, sugar, syrup, and cream in a pan.
  4. Remove the pan from the stove and mix in the oat mix.
  5. Slice the apples and spread out in a buttered pie form.
  6. Spread the mixture over the apples.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes or until the pie has a golden and crunchy crust.
  8. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
