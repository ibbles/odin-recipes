Sticky Chocolate Cake

Description

Classical Swedish Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 dl granulated sugar
  • 2.5 dl flour
  • 4 tbsp cacao
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 2 pinches salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Butter a springform pan, 21 cm diameter, and sprinkle with crumbs.
  3. Melt butter on low heat and let cool.
  4. Whip eggs and sugar slightly fluffy and add the butter.
  5. Mix flour, cacao, vanilla sugar, and salt. Add to the egg mix and stir until smooth.
  6. Add the batter to the pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes, it should be slightly runny.
  7. Serve with whipped cream and strawberries.
