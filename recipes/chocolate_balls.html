Chocolate Balls

Description

Classical Swedish chocolate balls according to the brand Gyllenhammars.

Ingredients

  • 100 g room temperature butter
  • 1 dl granulated sugar
  • 3 dl rolled oats
  • 2 msk cacao
  • 1 tsk vanilla sugar
  • 2 msk cold coffee
  • 2 dl nib sugar
  1. Mix all ingredients well, except for nib sugar.
  2. Form small balls, and cover in nib sugar.
  3. Store cold until set.
